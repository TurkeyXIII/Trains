TRAIN TRAX SERIES
=================

A set of high quality modular train track pieces and buildings, create your own amazing tracks in minutes! Includes a free game ready train model.

Track grid size is 16m square; just slot the track pieces together. All models are optimised for mobile, with a low number of materials.

Everything shown in the screenshots is included, including two example demo track scenes.

Note: Demo scene uses the Unity character controller. ProGrids 2 was used to place the track pieces in the demos.
